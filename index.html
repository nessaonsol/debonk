Promote Coin

An experiment to test if digital ads actually work

The Idea

We created a cryptocurrency that exists for one purpose: to be promoted through digital advertising. Every transaction fee goes directly into buying ads on Google, YouTube, and TikTok.

YouTube

TikTok

This lets us measure exactly how effective digital advertising really is by tracking ad spend versus actual purchases.

How It Works

  1. Promote Coin launches with dev bags (pre-allocated tokens)
  2. People buy and trade the token on exchanges
  3. All transaction fees are automatically collected
  4. 100% of fees fund advertising campaigns across platforms
  5. All updates, spending, and results posted on our X account
Complete Transparency: The coin launches with dev bags to ensure immediate liquidity. Every dollar in fees collected goes directly to advertising - no team allocation, no hidden costs. All campaign updates, spending reports, and results will be posted live on our X (Twitter) account.

Real-Time Updates

Follow our progress on X (Twitter) for live updates on ad spend, campaign performance, and conversion data. We'll post screenshots of ad dashboards, spending receipts, and detailed analytics as the experiment runs.

The Goal

Create the first real-world case study of digital advertising effectiveness using cryptocurrency mechanics. No speculation, just measurable results.

This is an experimental research project. Cryptocurrency carries risk. Only participate with money you can afford to lose.
